Matthew Velez graduated from his Bachelor of Fine Arts, Design at YSDN in Toronto in 2016. Since then, he's been developing art and creative work across multiple arenas.

His iconoclastic tastes along with his tenacity for problem solving found a fit in strategy and design. His personal work explores belonging, identity and loneliness, training a lens on our behaviour to encouraging self-reflection.

In 2019, he founded Untitled Design Studio with his partner, offering strategy and design services to the globe. Today he lives in Montreal with his partner, pursuing a career in Game Development while dipping his fingers into creative writing, music production, apparel & graphic design, UI, 3D art, 2D art and game design.
